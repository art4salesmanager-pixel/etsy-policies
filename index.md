# Privacy Policy — Modiqo

Welcome! This Privacy Policy explains what personal information I collect through my Etsy shop, how I use it, and what rights you have. I've kept things as simple and jargon-free as possible.

---

## 1. What Information I Collect

I do not collect data independently — Etsy shares the following with me when you place an order or send a message:

- **Name** — so I know who to address
- **Shipping address** — to send your order
- **Email address** — for order-related communication
- **Messages** — conversations you start through Etsy Messages
- **Order history** — items purchased, quantities, dates

I do not collect payment details. All payments are processed securely by Etsy and its payment partners — I never see your card or bank information.

---

## 2. How I Use Your Information

I use your data only for:

- **Fulfilling your order** — preparing, packing, and shipping your purchase
- **Customer support** — answering your questions and resolving any issues
- **Marketing** — only if you have explicitly opted in. I will not add you to any mailing list without your clear consent, and you can withdraw that consent at any time

I do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

---

## 3. Who I Share Your Information With

I share your information only where necessary:

- **Etsy** — as the platform through which orders are placed; Etsy's own Privacy Policy applies to how they handle your data
- **Postal and courier services** — your name and shipping address are shared with the carrier to deliver your order
- **Payment processors** — handled entirely by Etsy; I do not have access to your payment details

I do not share your data with advertisers, marketing platforms, or any other third parties.

---

## 4. How Long I Keep Your Data

- **Etsy** retains order data for up to **6 years** in line with their platform policies and legal requirements
- On my end, I keep only what is necessary for the duration of your order and any follow-up support, and no longer than legally required
- If you request deletion of your data, I will remove anything I hold directly as soon as reasonably possible (see Section 5)

---

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you have the following rights over your personal data.

**If you are in the EU or UK (GDPR, Articles 15–17):**

- **Right to access** — you can ask what data I hold about you
- **Right to rectification** — you can ask me to correct inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure** — you can ask me to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to restriction** — you can ask me to limit how I use your data
- **Right to object** — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact me directly (see Section 6). I will respond within 30 days.

**If you are in California (CCPA):**

- You have the right to know what personal information is collected and why
- You have the right to request deletion of your personal information
- You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information — I do not sell personal data
- You will not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

To submit a CCPA request, contact me at the details in Section 6.

---

## 6. Newsletter and Marketing Emails

If you sign up for updates from Modiqo outside of Etsy (for example, through a sign-up form on a website or social media), here is how I handle that:

**What I collect for the newsletter:**
- Your email address
- Your first name (if you provide it, so I can address you personally)

**What I send:**
- Shop news, new product announcements, and occasional promotions
- I aim to keep emails infrequent and relevant — no spam

**Your consent matters:**
- You will only receive marketing emails if you have actively opted in (ticked a checkbox or submitted a sign-up form)
- I will never add you to a mailing list based on a purchase alone
- Every email includes an **unsubscribe link** — one click and you're off the list, no questions asked

**Your right to unsubscribe:**
- You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email, or by contacting me directly at art4sales.manager@gmail.com
- After unsubscribing, I will remove your email from the list within **5 business days**

Unsubscribing from marketing emails does not affect order confirmations or support messages — those are sent only when needed to fulfil your order.

If you have any questions about this policy or want to exercise your rights, please reach out:

- **Email:** art4sales.manager@gmail.com
- **Etsy Messages:** via my shop page on Etsy

I aim to respond to all inquiries within **3 business days**.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If I update this Privacy Policy, I will notify you by:

- Sending an email to customers who have opted in to communications, and/or
- Updating this page with a new "Last updated" date

I encourage you to check back occasionally. Continued use of my shop after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
